About ResQChain

ResQChain is an AI-driven disaster response platform designed to reduce panic and guide civilians toward immediate help.

Using machine learning, live APIs, and geospatial intelligence, ResQChain helps people quickly find nearby shelters, hospitals, medicine shops, and evacuation routes during emergencies.

Built for hackathons under AI for Social Good, the system prioritizes human safety, speed, and clarity over complexity.

How It Works

1. Analyze

AI models analyze weather, disaster alerts, and location data in real time.

2. Locate

Nearby shelters, hospitals, and medicine shops are ranked by distance and need.

3. Respond

Civilians request help, shelters respond, and relief status is tracked live.
